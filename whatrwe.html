— What we are

Not a software house. Not a fund. A consultancy that plants.

Green Wadi is a small, independent consultancy. We build tools because the market does not; we keep them small because complexity kills farms.

— Manifesto

We believe agriculture is the slowest form of intelligence — a hundred-year craft that rewards patience, proof and restraint. Technology is our tool. The land is the client.

— Values

Four values, arranged around the land.

Every decision — a hire, a tool, a client, a protocol — is tested against these four. If it fails one, it does not happen.

— Structure

A partnership of 24 people, on purpose.

We will never grow past forty. Seniors work directly with clients. There are no junior account managers and no sales team.

— Governance

Employee-owned, independently audited.

100% held by the partners who do the work. Accounts audited annually by a Big Four firm; the full report is shared with every client.
