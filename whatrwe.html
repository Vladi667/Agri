— What we are

Agricultural Development Excellence Is What We Are.

Green Wadi is a focused, independent agricultural consultancy — specialists in turning agronomic potential into verifiable, long-term results for farmers, investors and governments.

— Our mission

Green Wadi will increase global agricultural production to mitigate hunger and malnutrition, pave the way for food self-sufficiency and security; strengthen the rural community and improve its livelihood, while protecting the environment and natural resources.

— Six principles

The principles that define every engagement.

These are not values statements. They are the operating principles we apply to every mandate, from a single farm diagnosis to a national rural development program.

01
Agricultural practices & agro-technology

Developing and introducing suitable agricultural practices, agro-technology, and post-harvest technology tailored to the specific conditions of each client.

02
Enhancing & building capacity

Enhancing and building capacity at all levels of the agricultural value chain — from the farmer in the field to the manager at the board level.

03
Results & success-oriented approach

Adopting a results and success-oriented approach. Every recommendation is measured, verified independently, and revisited at year five.

04
Customer satisfaction

Focusing on customer satisfaction in every interaction. We provide quick answers and solutions — not reports that sit on a shelf.

05
Long-term commitment

Making a long-term commitment to our customers and their projects. We are present in year one, year five, and year ten of every engagement.

06
Optimal tailor-made solutions

Providing optimal tailor-made solutions for any given condition. No two farms, no two markets, no two challenges are ever identical.

Four values, arranged around the land.
