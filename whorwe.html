— Who we are

A consultancy of agronomists, data scientists and patient operators.

Founded in 2014 between Muscat and Lisbon, Green Wadi exists to serve families, institutions and governments who believe that agriculture is a hundred-year craft — and that technology, deployed with restraint, is how we keep it that way.

— Our philosophy

Small decisions, compounded across seasons.

We resist single-season heroics. Every recommendation is tested on-farm, measured independently, and revisited five years later. If a protocol doesn't hold up at year five, it doesn't ship.

— Our journey

A decade of patient fieldwork.

2014
Founded in Muscat

Three agronomists and one data scientist set up shop with a single commitment: five-year reviews on every engagement.

2017
First 100 farms

Cereal, olive and date palm clients across the Arabian peninsula. The soil-telemetry platform ships v1.

2020
Desertification practice

A partnership with three sovereign funds brings drylands rehabilitation into the core offering.

2023
Aquaculture & forestry

Integrated multi-trophic aquaculture joins the catalogue. Agroforestry becomes a standalone practice.

2026
184 farms, 12 countries

Independent auditors verify a 47% average yield uplift and a 38% reduction in water use.

— Principles

Four principles we will not negotiate.

No proprietary lock-in

Clients own their data, their models, their soil. We leave — the knowledge stays.

Independent audit

Every yield and water claim is verified by a third party before it leaves our offices.

Native first

Local cultivars, local species, local hands. Imports are a last resort, never a default.

Generational horizon

Our plans run to year 100. We bill for a decade and consult for a lifetime.
