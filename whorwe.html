— Our story

From one agronomist to a global practice.

Green Wadi Ltd. started as a one-man operation when Mr. Friedman left the Ministry of Agriculture of Israel to become an independent consultant 10 years ago.

In 2018, due to the expansion of projects, Green Wadi became a limited company employing experts as needed to be efficient and successful. We are committed to providing our clients with quick answers and solutions.

Private investors, governments, and development funds around the world have trusted Green Wadi to realize their agricultural and rural development vision.

We offer diverse services, technology, know-how, and training required for the upgrading and construction of existing and new agricultural and agro-industrial enterprises.

Our successful projects have earned Green Wadi a reputation for delivering measurable value for the long term.

— Our journey

A decade of patient fieldwork.

From a single consultant to a trusted partner of sovereign funds and private investors across three continents.

2014
Founded in Israel

Shmuel Friedman leaves the Ministry of Agriculture of Israel to establish an independent agricultural consultancy — Green Wadi.

2016
First international projects

Green Wadi expands beyond Israel, bringing Israeli agro-technology and expertise to clients across the region.

2018
Incorporated as Green Wadi Ltd.

Due to expansion of projects, Green Wadi became a limited company employing domain experts as needed to be efficient and successful.

2020
Rural development & food security

Governments and development funds begin partnering with Green Wadi for national-scale food security and rural development programs.

2024
184 farms · 12 countries

Green Wadi now operates across 12 countries, having transformed over 184 agricultural enterprises with measurable, lasting results.
